## Notes

- Do not send to no-reply addresses.
- Do not use a service role key, private account password, or any website admin secret in outreach.
- Google for Nonprofits may require legal nonprofit registration details in Jordan.
- Microsoft and Google may require official organization verification before granting nonprofit benefits.
- Keep replies in the same email thread for tracking.
